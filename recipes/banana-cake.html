Banana Cake recipe

Description

Rich Banana Cake recipe.

Ideal for family and friend gatherings!.

Ingredients

Cake:

  • Mashed bananas
  • Lemon juice
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • White sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Buttermilk

Frosting:

  • Softened butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Vanilla extract
  • Confectioner's sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degree F
  2. Mix mashed bananas with lemon juice
  3. Mix flour, baking soda and salt
  4. Cream sugar and butter in large bowl
  5. Beat in eggs, stir in vanilla
  6. Beat in flour
  7. Pour batter in prepared pan
  8. Bake in preheated oven
  9. Remove cake and freeze for 45 minutes
  10. Make frosting
  11. Spread frosting in cooled cake
  12. Serve and enjoy!
