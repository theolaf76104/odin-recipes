Banana Cake recipe

Description

Rich Banana Cake.

Ideal for family and friend gatherings!.

Ingredients

Cake:

  • Mashed bananas
  • Lemon juice
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • White sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Buttermilk

Frosting:

  • Softened butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Vanilla extract
  • Confectioner's sugar
