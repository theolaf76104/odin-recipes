Oatmeal Cookie Apple Crisp recipe

Description

This Oatmeal Apple Crisp recipe is crunchy, chewy cookie with buttery soft apples flavourings

Great for a party!

Ingredients

Filling:

  • Peeled and sliced apples
  • White sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Salt

Topping:

  • Rolled oats
  • All-purpose flour
  • Brown sugar
  • Baking soda
  • Baking powder
  • Melted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix apples, white sugar, tbs flour, cinnamon and salt
  3. Combine oats, flour, brown sugar, baking soda and baking powder
  4. Bake in preheated oven, 30 to 45 minutes
